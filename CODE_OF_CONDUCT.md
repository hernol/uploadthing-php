# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all contributors, regardless of:

- Age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression
- Level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality
- Personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation

## Our Standards

### Positive Behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language
- Be respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accept constructive criticism
- Focus on what is best for the community
- Show empathy towards other community members

### Unacceptable Behavior

- The use of sexualized language or imagery
- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Enforcement

### Reporting

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project team at **conduct@uploadthing.com**. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated.

### Enforcement Guidelines

Project maintainers will:

1. **Clarify**: Provide clear explanation of the behavior and why it's inappropriate
2. **Warning**: Issue a warning for first-time, minor violations
3. **Temporary Ban**: Temporarily ban from community for repeated violations
4. **Permanent Ban**: Permanently ban for severe or repeated violations

### Enforcement Process

1. **Report**: Community members report violations
2. **Investigate**: Project team investigates the report
3. **Respond**: Appropriate action is taken based on investigation
4. **Follow-up**: Follow-up with all parties involved
5. **Document**: Document the incident and resolution

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- Project spaces (GitHub, issues, pull requests)
- Public spaces when representing the project
- Community events and meetups
- Online discussions and communications

For questions about this Code of Conduct, please contact:

- **Email**: conduct@uploadthing.com
- **Response Time**: Within 48 hours
- **Confidentiality**: All reports are treated confidentially

## Updates

This Code of Conduct may be updated from time to time. Changes will be communicated to the community and documented in the project's changelog.
